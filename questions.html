Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for Interlock System installation?

To have your Interlock System installed, you need to provide a valid Colorado identification card and proof of vehicle registration.

What do I need to apply for a restricted license?

To apply for a restricted license, you will need to provide the following documents: a SR22, Interlock Affidavit, and proof of Interlock System installation.

How long is the average requirement for having the Interlock System installed?

95% percent of our clients are required to have the system in their vehicle for two years. However, under special circumstance, a client may be eligible for early release after 120 days.
